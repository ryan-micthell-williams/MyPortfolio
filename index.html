| MORE ABOUT ME |

All my life, I have possessed an insatiable desire to learn and expand my horizons both intellectually and professionally.

The internet has allowed me to satisfy innumerable curiosities and discover reality-warping revelations that fundamentally alter the way I think and perceive the world around me. For anyone with access to the web, the sum total of human knowledge is right there at their fingertips.

Computers have enabled me to bring my imagination to life through video production and special effects software. I want every man, woman, and child to have economically-inclusive access to high-quality digital tools that allow them to create without any limits except their own vision. I seek to realize a dream encapsulated quite poetically in a quote from a deeply flawed yet brilliant crusader of computational innovation, the late Steve Jobs,

[T]he computer is the most remarkable tool that we've ever come up with. It's the equivalent of a bicycle for our minds. What if, instead of being in the right hands, it was in everyone's hands? Everyone in the world.
