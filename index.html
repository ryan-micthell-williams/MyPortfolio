| MORE ABOUT ME |

All my life, I have possessed an insatiable desire to learn and expand my horizons both intellectually and professionally. I aim to share my passion for creation and goal-oriented workflow with my coworkers, thereby creating an atmosphere of inclusivity and positivity that translates into exceptional job performance and productivity.

I'm the guy who never gives up on my teammates or on our projects. No matter how impossible, insurmountable or daunting a challenge might seem, I will always find a way for me and my colleagues to get the job done.

Computers have enabled me to bring my imagination to life through video production and special effects software. They allow me to transport myself to impossibly fantastical and intricate worlds that could never exist in our own reality. I want every man, woman, and child to have economically-inclusive access to high-quality digital tools that allow them to create without any limits except their own vision. I seek to realize a dream encapsulated quite poetically in a quote from a deeply flawed yet brilliant crusader of computational innovation, the late Steve Jobs,

[T]he computer is the most remarkable tool that we've ever come up with. It's the equivalent of a bicycle for our minds. What if, instead of being in the right hands, it was in everyone's hands? Everyone in the world.
