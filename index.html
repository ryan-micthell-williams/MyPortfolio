local library project

the goal:

Build the algorithms necessary for a local library to keep track of general statistics including which books are available, which are presently checked-out, which users have which books, which books are overdue, etc.

decoder ring project

the goal:

Create several complex algorithms to emulate several notable encryption and decryption systems used throughout history such as the Caesar Shift, the Polybius Square, and the Substitution Cipher.

| the man behind the monitor |

All my life, I have possessed an insatiable desire to learn and expand my horizons both intellectually and professionally.

Before I began my quest to become a software engineer under the tutelage of the wonderful minds at Thinkful, I found myself pursuing a career in medicine. Unfortunately, that journey was put on an indefinite hold due to the ever-increasing severity of the COVID-19 pandemic.

But the show must go on, and I have never been one to linger in stagnant waters for very long. In the indefinite interim between my departure and my uncertain return to ultrasound school, I felt compelled to satisfy my technological curiosity and pursue a career in another field that greatly interested me. The tech industry enables the most potent and immediate change within our society. If I can't save lives through healthcare, then why not drastically improve people's lives with digital creations that foster the best aspects of human ingenuity. I seek to realize a dream encapsulated quite poetically in a quote from a deeply flawed yet brilliant crusader of computational innovation, the late Steve Jobs,

[T]he computer is the most remarkable tool that we've ever come up with. It's the equivalent of a bicycle for our minds. What if, instead of being in the right hands, it was in everyone's hands? Everyone in the world.
